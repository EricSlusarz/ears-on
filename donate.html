Support our cause by giving a chartiable donation. All procedes go directly to kids with hearing impairment.

Be one of the thousands of people who donate there hearing aids every year. Affordable hearing aids help those who want to hear and experience the world to the fullest. The process of donation takes time but it is worth it in the end when poverty stricken individuals are given the gift of sound.

Donate Your Hearing Devices Today

Follow these 3 easy steps to give your hearing aid to children in need.

  1. Secure your hearing device in a well padded and sturdy container.
  2. Mail to: Ears On at 45 W Lane Ave Columbus, OH 43201
  3. If you would like a donation recepit please send your name and address so we can send you a copy.
  4. Please include your name and address where you would like a donation receipt sent by filling out the Downloadable Donation Card.
